What Your Partnership Funds

Program Access

League fees, equipment, and campus visit travel — removed as barriers so every student can participate regardless of family income. We fund access so kids can focus on the work.

College Access Information

The No-Loan College Database, college guidance sessions, and funded campus visits where students meet directly with academic departments — not just tour guides. We show them where they’ll actually spend their time.

Mentorship Infrastructure

Streamlining coaching resources, admin best practices, and success pathways so organizations can focus on the kids. Coaches clinics, shared support, and spotlights of success across the mountain community.
