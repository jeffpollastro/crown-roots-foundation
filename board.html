Board & Leadership

The people building pathways for Pocono Mountain youth — through athletics, academics, and mentorship.

Foundation Staff

Crown Roots Foundation is led by a team with deep roots in basketball, youth development, education, and creative strategy.

Wayne Jones

Co-Founder & Executive Director

Wayne Jones brings over three decades of experience in basketball coaching, player development, mentorship, and program management. He is the President and Founder of Bigtime Sports Academy and Bigtime Sports and Adventures, where he has established premier platforms for elite player development, college recruitment support, and transformative life experiences for student-athletes and teens. Wayne previously served as Associate Head Coach at New York Institute of Technology, where he played a key role in transforming the program into a regional powerhouse, achieving two NCAA Tournament appearances and a Sweet 16 run, while producing multiple All-ECC players and a Conference Player of the Year.

As Co-Founder and Executive Director of Crown Roots Foundation, Wayne brings his proven expertise in developing high-performing teams, cultivating leadership both on and off the court, and building scalable programs that integrate athletic excellence with academic success. A passionate advocate for youth mentorship and community-based development, Wayne authored How To Build A GIANT, a blueprint for developing successful basketball feeder teams and community programs. He is committed to creating pathways for underserved young people to achieve their full potential through athletics, academics, and purposeful leadership.

Jeff Pollastro

Co-Founder & Creative Art Director

Jeff Pollastro brings a wide-ranging background spanning creative direction, nonprofit communications, high-end interiors, basketball coaching, and youth mentorship. He has worked across multiple disciplines — as a decorative painter, creative strategist, and program designer — with a consistent focus on connecting academics, art, and athletics for young people. His philosophy centers on using data and technology strategically: tech tools are valuable only insomuch as they provide access and opportunity to those who need it most.

As Co-Founder and Creative Director of Crown Roots Foundation, Jeff leads the design and scaling of youth programs across the Poconos with plans for national expansion. He works directly with schools and community partners to expand access and opportunity, designs programs grounded in evidence, coaches and mentors young people, and leverages data and analytics to measure impact and improve outcomes. His vision is building a regional resource ecosystem that transforms how young people experience education, athletics, and opportunity.

Board of Directors

Crown Roots Foundation is governed by a Board of Directors responsible for strategic oversight, fiduciary stewardship, and ensuring the organization stays true to its mission.

Dr. Winston Wallace

Board Member
Executive Director, A Vision of a Better Tomorrow

Dr. Winston Wallace brings over 20 years of progressive experience in human services, from direct support roles to executive leadership. He holds five degrees in human services, including a Ph.D. in Psychology, and has completed extensive clinical training. His career spans multiple roles — clinician, program manager, clinical consultant — and includes positions with major service providers across New York, Georgia, and Pennsylvania. He has worked with diverse populations including youth with behavioral and emotional challenges, adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities, and co-occurring disorders.

As Executive Director of A Vision of a Better Tomorrow, Dr. Wallace leads an organization dedicated to providing quality support services, education, resources, and mentorship to people with intellectual and developmental disabilities and underserved populations. As a member of the Crown Roots Foundation board, Dr. Wallace brings this same commitment to creating a culture rooted in competency, empathy, and integrity, where underserved communities have pathways to opportunity, resources, and purpose.

Luis Ramirez

Board Member
Upper School Principal, International Community School of Abidjan

Luis Ramirez is an accomplished educational leader with 15+ years of progressive international school leadership experience across Latin America, the Middle East, and Asia. He currently serves as Upper School Principal at the International Community School of Abidjan and has held leadership positions including Principal at American Pacific International School and Secondary Director at the American International School of Bolivia. Luis is a dedicated International Baccalaureate (IB) volunteer, site visitor, and consultant with nearly 15 years of impact supporting IB programs and students worldwide.

A transformative leader known for fostering effective communication, maximizing team potential, and engaging diverse collaborative stakeholders, Luis brings deep expertise in secondary education, organizational leadership, and international educational strategy. As a member of the Crown Roots Foundation board, Luis is committed to expanding access to quality education and opportunity for underserved young people.

Terrence Bomar

Board Member
Founder, Monroe Inspired Foundation

Terrence Bomar is an accomplished educational leader with progressive experience across K-12 and higher education. Currently serving as Assistant Principal at Pocono Mountain West High School, Terrence previously held leadership positions as Assistant Director of Admissions at Northampton Community College and Assistant Principal with the East Stroudsburg Area School District. He brings extensive coaching experience as Head Women's Basketball Coach at Northampton Community College, where he earned Coach of the Year honors (2022–23) and led his team to a Region 19 NJCAA Championship.

Beyond institutional roles, Terrence is a passionate community builder and founder of Monroe Inspired Foundation, a youth development initiative serving the local community. He currently sits on the board for the local basketball nonprofit, Perryman Keglovits All Star Basketball Game, and actively serves as a PIAA referee for high school and youth athletics. As a member of the Crown Roots Foundation board, Terrence brings expertise in school partnerships, youth athletics, and community-based program development.

Demetria Cooley

Board Member
School Therapist, Pocono Mountain School District

Demetria Cooley is a licensed school therapist and trauma specialist currently serving students at Pocono Mountain West schools, where she provides mental health support, behavioral intervention, and trauma-informed care as part of the school's Behavioral Health department. Prior to joining Pocono Mountain, she built her clinical foundation at The MENTOR Network, a national human services organization, where she served first as an Autism Specialist working with individuals on the spectrum in the Bethlehem, PA area, and then as a Clinical Coordinator overseeing care delivery and clinical operations.

With deep roots in the Pocono community and hands-on experience supporting young people through behavioral challenges, trauma, and developmental needs, Demetria brings a critical lens to the Crown Roots Foundation board. Her expertise ensures the organization's programming is grounded in the social-emotional realities facing the students it serves.

Padraig O'Donoghue

Board Member
CUNY School of Labor and Urban Studies

Padraig O'Donoghue has served as Manager of Student Support and Retention at the City University of New York since 2014, where he leads student services at the CUNY School of Labor and Urban Studies. His career in higher education spans student recruitment, admissions, academic advising, and outreach — including roles as a recruiter, admissions counselor, and outreach coordinator for the Public Administration program at the Murphy Institute, and earlier as a writing tutor and English instructor at Hunter College.

He holds a degree in Writing for Film and Media from the CUNY Baccalaureate for Unique and Interdisciplinary Studies program. A native of Ireland, Padraig brings over a decade of experience guiding underserved and nontraditional students through the systems of higher education — expertise that directly informs Crown Roots Foundation's mission of connecting young people to academic and life opportunity.
