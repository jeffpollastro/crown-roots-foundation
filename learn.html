Pocono families leave thousands in scholarships unclaimed every year — not because they don’t qualify, but because they don’t know where to look.

Time-Sensitive — PA Families: Pennsylvania’s State Grant through PHEAA must be triggered by filing your FAFSA by May 1. The grant is portable — it can be used at approved out-of-state schools, not only Pennsylvania colleges. Filing late significantly reduces eligibility. Learn more at PHEAA.org →
